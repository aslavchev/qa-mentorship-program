SMOKE-001: Application loads successfully
- Verify that the SauceDemo homepage loads without errors.

SMOKE-002: Login succeeds
- Login with valid credentials (standard_user / secret_sauce) succeeds.

SMOKE-003: Products display
- Verify that the product catalog displays all products after login.

SMOKE-004: Add to cart works
- Add a product to the cart and verify that cart count increases.

SMOKE-005: Cart page accessible
- Navigate to the cart page and ensure it loads correctly.

SMOKE-006: Checkout accessible
- Proceed to checkout and verify that checkout steps appear.

SMOKE-007: Logout works
- Logout from the application and verify redirect to login page.

SMOKE-008: Remove from cart works
- Remove a product from the cart and verify cart count decreases.

SMOKE-009: Checkout complete
- Complete the checkout process and verify the confirmation page.

SMOKE-010: Error message for invalid login
- Enter invalid credentials and verify that an error message is displayed.
